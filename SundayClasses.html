Sunday Feast & Bhagavatam Classes

Join us every Sunday for spiritual enlightenment and divine prasadam

View Schedule Sponser Sunday Feast

Spiritual Nourishment Every Sunday

Our Sunday program combines profound spiritual wisdom with delicious sanctified food

Bhagavatam Class

Engage in the timeless wisdom of Srimad Bhagavatam with our senior devotees. Learn practical applications of spiritual knowledge in daily life.

Love Feast

Partake in delicious vegetarian prasadam prepared with devotion and offered to Lord Krishna. Experience the taste of spiritual food.

Kirtan

Join in the congregational chanting of the holy names with melodious kirtan that purifies the heart and mind.

Weekly Sunday Schedule

Our regular Sunday program timing (subject to change during festivals)

4:30 AM

Mangala Arati

Begin the day with the beautiful ceremony of deity worship

5:00 AM - 5:15 AM

Tulsi Puja

Offering prayers and devotion to sacred Tulsi Devi for spiritual blessings and purity.

5:15 AM - 7:00 AM

Japa Meditation

A peaceful practice of chanting the holy names to awaken devotion and inner clarity.

7:30 AM - 8:00 AM

Guru Puja & Kirtan

Honoring the spiritual master with love, gratitude, and devotional offerings.

7:30 AM - 8:00 AM

Srimad Bhagavatam class

Hearing divine wisdom that nourishes the soul and deepens spiritual understanding.

10:00 AM - 10:30 AM

Ecstatic Kirtan

Joyful singing of the Lord’s names that fills the heart with devotion and bliss.

10:30 AM

Sunday Love Feast

Delight in Krishna prasadam — sacred food offered to the Lord, nourishing both body and soul with divine grace.
